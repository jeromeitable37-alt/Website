About the Office

The Guidance Office is dedicated to supporting students in their academic, personal, social, and career development through professional counseling and guidance services.

Mission

To provide compassionate, confidential guidance services that support students' academic success, personal growth, and emotional well-being.

Vision

A school community where all students feel valued, supported, and equipped to pursue their dreams confidently.

Role

To assist students through counseling, career guidance, and collaboration with parents and teachers.

History

Established in 2005, the office has grown from a small counseling center to a comprehensive support hub, adapting to student needs over the years.

Policies

We maintain strict confidentiality, promote anti-bullying, and ensure inclusivity for all students regardless of background.
